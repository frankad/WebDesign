About Us

North West Beach Park is a lovely grassy wetland parks on the shores of Lake Saints, south of the Evergreen Mountain. North Beach Park provides welcoming and safe opportunities to play, learn, contemplate and build community, and promotes responsible environmental management of the land. It promote ecological balance, healthy environment, healthy people and strong communities. It covers 7,000 acre natural areas.

Mission Of The Park

The park preserves and improves natural and cultural resources, and provides educational, recreational, and research opportunities to the community. It increase public awareness, knowledge and understanding of the inherent values of wetlands, and to organize public support and action for wetland conservation. It can also provide ecotourism facility to serve both local residents and overseas tourists.
