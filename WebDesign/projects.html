Why The Park Important?

The park has wetlands, which is highly productive and biologically diverse systems that control erosion, improve water quality, balance the ecosystem, and provide a home to all threatened and endangered species.

Current Projects

Thanks to many generous supporters and partners with the North Beach Park, the park has completed many major improvement project. We look forward to providing better facilities for the communities and provide many educational and recreational opportunities. Currently, we are working on the following projects.

  1. Floood Control and Streamflow Maintenance
  2. Streambank Stabilization and Erosion Control
  3. Expand Ecotourism and Rcreational Opportunities

Education

It provides educational opportunities for the surrounding community because the park has ecological, cultural, and historic resources. So, it provide a lot of opportunities for environmental education and public awareness programs.
